Session

warm-up

Before we start any kind of session be sure to take a 10-15 minute warm-up. I usually begin with 10 minutes of easier jogging or powerwalk, followed by a couple of movement exercises to get the body limber and ready to go. Below I've posted three different kind of sessions, but you can easily pair the exercises in any other way.

Hypertrophy Focus

Exercise Sets Repetitions Rest between sets
Deadlift 3 11 90 seconds
Squat 3 11 90 seconds
Calf Raise 3 15 90 seconds
Hip Thruster 3 11 90 seconds

Strength development

Exercise Sets Repetitions Rest between sets
Deadlift 4 5-6 120-150 seconds
Squat 4 5-6 120-150 seconds
Calf Raise 4 6-8 120-150 seconds
Hip Thruster 4 5-6 120-150 seconds
